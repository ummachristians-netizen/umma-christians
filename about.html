Who We Are

Umma Christian Union is a student-led fellowship that serves as a spiritual home for Christian students at Umma University. We gather for worship, biblical teaching, prayer, discipleship, and practical service.

Our Calling

To raise a generation of students who walk with Christ, demonstrate godly character, and influence every sphere of campus life.

Our Approach

Through consistent fellowship, pastoral guidance, mentorship, and ministry teams, students are equipped to grow spiritually and serve faithfully.
